Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your pricing different from other processors?

We offer direct interchange-plus pricing with complete transparency. You'll see every fee clearly listed, and we guarantee no hidden charges or surprise fees on your statement.

How quickly can I start processing payments?

Most merchants can be approved and processing within 24-48 hours. Our streamlined application process and dedicated support team ensure a smooth and quick setup.

What security measures do you have in place?

We maintain PCI Level 1 compliance, employ end-to-end encryption, and use advanced fraud detection systems. Our security protocols exceed industry standards to protect your business and customers.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. We believe in earning your business every month. Our services are month-to-month with no early termination fees or long-term commitments required.

What types of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 local support via phone, email, and chat. You'll have a dedicated account manager and access to our technical team for integration assistance.
